Our Story

At FreshMix, we believe in bringing the natural goodness of fruits to your daily life. Our journey began in 2018 with a simple vision: to create a place where people could enjoy the freshest, most delicious fruit cocktails, crepes, ice creams, and slushes in a welcoming environment.

Every item on our menu is crafted with care, using only the highest quality ingredients. Our fruits are locally sourced whenever possible, and we prepare everything fresh daily to ensure you get the best flavor and nutritional value in every bite and sip.
